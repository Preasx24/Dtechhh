About Us

Welcome to D-TECH Headquarters

Founded by visionary innovator @preasx24, D-TECH is an ecosystem of digital innovation committed to education, empowerment, and solving everyday tech problems. @preasx24 is the mind behind D-TECH Services, D-TECH Info, D-TECH HQ, and D-TECH General—a growing network designed to serve students, creators, coders, and communities across Africa and beyond.

What is D-TECH?

D-TECH stands for Dynamic Technology, a movement led by @preasx24 that reimagines how tech is built, taught, and shared. From practical tools to powerful automation scripts, the D-TECH vision is one of limitless innovation. D-TECH consists of four main pillars:

  • D-TECH Services – A student-centered platform for renting, reselling, and exchanging goods.
  • D-TECH Info – An online tech and learning hub providing tutorials, tips, and digital tools.
  • D-TECH HQ – The central brain of the brand, where ideas are developed and high-tech solutions are born.
  • D-TECH General – A centralized platform showcasing all D-TECH services and resources.

Our Mission

Led by @preasx24, our mission is to build systems that:

  • Empower youth through accessible tech education.
  • Deliver creative, affordable solutions to real-world problems.
  • Foster a network of learners, hackers, and visionaries.
  • Innovate at the intersection of code, commerce, and community.
  • Lay the foundation for the future D-TECH Headquarters City, a physical home for innovation and collaboration.

Key Projects by @preasx24
