Meet @Preasx24

@Preasx24 is the founder and creative leader behind @DTECH — a groundbreaking ecosystem that integrates student empowerment, innovation, and tech-driven solutions. Through platforms like DTECH Services, DTECH Info, DTECH HQ, and DTECH General, @Preasx24 is changing the way students connect with digital tools, opportunities, and each other.

As a rising name in South African digital entrepreneurship, @Preasx24 has positioned DTECH not just as a brand, but as a community-driven initiative where young people, especially students, can thrive. From online marketplaces to education hubs and software innovation, @Preasx24 ensures each platform offers real-world value and long-term impact.
